Multimedia domain elements

The multimedia elements are used to reference audio or video content. The elements in this domain are modeled on the HTML5 <audio> and <video> elements.

  • audio
    Audio is sound that the human ear is capable of hearing.
  • media-autoplay
    Autoplay settings specify whether referenced media plays automatically.
  • media-controls
    Media control settings specify whether user-interface components are presented to control the playback of the referenced media.
  • media-loop
    Media loop settings specify whether the referenced media restarts automatically when it has completed playing.
  • media-muted
    Media mute settings specify whether the referenced media plays with or without sound.
  • media-source
    The media source specifies the location of an audio or video resource.
  • media-track
    Media track settings specify the location of supplemental text-based data for the referenced media, for example, subtitles or descriptions.
  • video
    A video is a recording of moving visual images.
  • video-poster
    Video poster settings specify the image that is rendered before video playback begins.
