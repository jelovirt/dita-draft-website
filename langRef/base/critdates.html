<critdates>

A <critdates> element specifies the critical dates in a document life cycle, such as the creation date and revision dates.

Usage information

This element is equivalent to the <Date> element in Dublin Core.

Attributes

The following attributes are available on this element: Universal attribute group.

Example

The following code sample shows that the topic was created on the twelth day of June in 2020, and updated on the 20th of August in 2020. 

<prolog>
  <critdates>
    <created date="2020-06-12"></created>
    <revised modified="2020-08-20"></revised>
  </critdates>
</prolog>
