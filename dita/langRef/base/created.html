<created>

The creation date is the date that a document was ceated.

Attributes

The following attributes are available on this element: universal attributes, date attributes, and the attribute defined below.

@date (REQUIRED)
Specifies the document creation date. The date is specified using the ISO 8601 format: YYYY-MM-DD, where YYYY is the year, MM is the month (01 to 12), and DD is the day (01-31).
